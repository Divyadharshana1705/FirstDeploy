Prevention & Solutions

Learn how to protect coconut trees from diseases.

Bud Rot

Cause: Fungus Phytophthora palmivora

Prevention: Use copper-based fungicides and proper drainage.

Leaf Blight

Cause: Fungal infection from Pestalotiopsis

Prevention: Prune affected leaves and apply fungicide spray.

Stem Bleeding

Cause: Thielaviopsis paradoxa fungus

Prevention: Avoid wounds on the trunk and use Bordeaux mixture.
